ABOUT ME

Hi There! I'm Pushkar Jajoria

Deep Learning Researcher

Hi, I'm Pushkar I am a Deep Learning researcher. I am currently working for the University of Galway on the Polifonia project.

My aim in life is to advance and use the methods of Deep learning to solve interesting and challenging problems. My current research is on Audio-Lyrics Alignment for Italian Arias using transfer learning. To this end, I create DL methods for modeling both symbolic and audio music which allows music composers, musicians, and musicologists to leverage these models.

I offer consultations for companies in picking the right and the best solution for their use case. Feel free to drop me a message to get in touch.

I like playing my guitar (Ibanez RG370) and composing music. I try to use machine learning models to help me compose better music.

I'll be happy to meet and discuss new ideas, and opportunities, or just have a casual chat over coffee.
